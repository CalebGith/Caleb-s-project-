Welcome to AMEU in Liberia!

AMEU, or African Methodist Episcopal University, is a prestigious institution located in Liberia. It offers a wide range of programs and degrees in various fields.

History of AMEU

AMEU was founded in 1995 with the mission to provide quality education and contribute to the development of Liberia. It has since become a leading educational institution in the country.

Programs Offered

  • Bachelor's degrees in Business, Education, and Health Sciences
  • Master's degrees in Public Administration and International Relations
  • Doctoral programs in various disciplines

Campus Facilities

AMEU boasts modern facilities, including state-of-the-art classrooms, libraries, laboratories, and sports facilities. The campus provides a vibrant learning environment for students.
